About Razorfish:

The SEO Director is a senior client management position in the organization responsible for leading a team of senior/junior account managers, as well as managing strategy, campaign management, analysis and P&L for our largest clients.

Job Description:

The SEO Director is a senior client management position in the organization responsible for leading a team of senior/junior account managers, as well as managing strategy, campaign management, analysis and P&L for our largest clients.

Responsibilities:

    • Managing client relationships, business needs assessments, search engine marketing efforts and client-level P&L for largest clients
    • Managing account management teams of 3 - 5 people, with ultimate accountability for integrated client results across all relevant products and services
    • Developing and implementing online search engine marketing strategies to achieve client objectives and supporting account management team 
    • Developing, executing and monitoring effective online marketing campaigns, specifically using search engines as the delivery vehicle for obtaining targeted leads and acquisitions
    • Performing in-depth quantitative analysis of search marketing campaigns and identifying more advanced analysis areas for analysts
    • Providing frequent progress reports regarding online marketing efforts and related performance metrics to clients
    • Contributing to organizational operations beyond basic job requirements (e.g., methodology development, recruiting, process redesign, tool improvements)

Qualifications:

  • 6 - 8 years of related industry experience with proven ability as a self-starter
  • 3+ years of online marketing, direct marketing and/or related strategy work including campaign development and performance analysis
  • 2+ years demonstrated track record of client relationship development and service, with a strong client-service orientation
  • Bachelor's Degree at a minimum, MBA is preferred
  • Direct experience in SEO, experience in SEM is a plus
  • Strong technical aptitude including some ability to understand basic website technologies
  • Sound business acumen, financial analysis skills, strategic skills and common sense
  • Demonstrated prowess in analyzing and interpreting complex data using advanced Excel
  • Proven ability to manage projects to successful completion, multi-task and work within tight deadlines
  • Track record of leadership, coaching and mentoring
  • Exceptional creative thinking and problem resolution skills that will contribute value in a dynamic, client-centered environment
  • Ability to work well in a team, to shine as an individual and to maintain a sense of humor

 

This is a full-time opportunity based in Atlanta.
